Overview

The 1st Hong Kong Computer Vision Workshop will be hosted at the Chinese University of Hong Kong on Sunday, October 13, 2019. This workshop is intended to bring together researchers in computer vision and related AI areas for an informal exchange of ideas and latest progress through a full day of presentations and posters.

In the workshop, faculty members and students from various universities and research labs in Hong Kong will share their recent ICCV work and discuss the future challenges in computer vision and AI. Participation is free for all the attendees and lunch will be provided. Prizes will be awarded to the best student presenters.
